About Me

My Journey

Hey there! I'm Sphoorthi Malladad, a graduate student in Information Systems at Kelley School of Business - IUB. I'm passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and bridge the gap between business needs and smart, scalable solutions. My interests lie at the intersection of data, systems design, and technology strategy - and I'm especially excited by work that blends analytical thinking with real impact.

Whether it's exploring a new city, capturing the perfect golden hour shot, or just unwinding with some good food and better company - I'm all about finding little joys along the way.
